    Privacy
    notice.

    How ESRF.net handles personal data — under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable member-state laws.

    Last updated: 17 April 2026.

    1. Who we are

    ESRF.net is the community platform of Stichting ESRF European Security and Resilience Fund, a not-for-profit organisation established in the European Union. For the purposes of the GDPR, Stichting ESRF European Security and Resilience Fund is the data controller for personal data processed through this website. Questions may be directed to privacy@esrf.net.

    2. What we collect

    We aim to process the minimum personal data necessary to operate the community. Categories may include:

    • Listing data — name, sector, city, country, website and description of organisations that request a listing.
    • Contact data — email addresses, names and role titles of individuals who write to us to request listings, submit dispatch items or contribute to the foundation.
    • Technical data — anonymised server logs (IP address truncated to remove the last octet, no user-agent stored) kept for security and rate-limiting only.

    We do not sell personal data.

    3. Why we process it

    Processing grounds under GDPR Article 6:

    • Legitimate interest — operating and improving the atlas, directory and dispatch.
    • Consent — when you explicitly opt in to receive correspondence from ESRF.net.
    • Legal obligation — where retention of certain records is required by law.

    4. How long we keep it

    Listing data is retained for as long as an organisation appears in the directory, plus a reasonable archival period. Contact data is retained while a conversation is active and for a maximum of twenty-four months thereafter, unless legal obligations require otherwise.

    5. Your rights

    Under the GDPR you may request access to, rectification of, erasure of or restriction on processing of your personal data, as well as data portability and objection. Write to privacy@esrf.net. You may also lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority.

    6. Cookies and advertising

    ESRF.net uses a minimal number of functional cookies necessary to operate the website. In addition, we display contextual advertisements through Google AdSense to support the foundation's mission. These advertisements may use cookies to serve relevant content. You may accept or decline advertising cookies through our consent banner — declining does not affect your access to any content on this site.

    We use Google's Consent Mode v2, which means no advertising data is collected until you explicitly consent. If you decline, no advertising cookies are set and no personal data is shared with advertising partners. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for esrf.net.

    7. Third parties

    We use a small number of trusted processors:

    • Cloudflare (hosting, serverless functions, KV storage) — EU data centres; GDPR-compliant DPA in place.
    • Resend (transactional email delivery, EU region eu-west-1) — processes only the data necessary to deliver form notification emails (sender, recipient, subject, body). Resend does not retain email content beyond delivery. Resend DPA.
    • CartoDB / OpenStreetMap (map tiles) — no personal data is shared.
    • Google AdSense (contextual advertising) — Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy. No personal data from our listings is shared with advertisers.

    All processors operate under GDPR-compliant terms. Form submission data is stored with automatic expiry (maximum 24 months) and IP addresses are truncated before storage to prevent individual identification.

    8. Changes

    We will post material changes to this notice on this page. Where a change is substantive, we will also attempt to inform listed organisations by email.
