Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

ESRF.net ("we", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. What We Collect

ESRF.net is a static informational website. We do not collect personal data through forms, accounts, or cookies. We may collect:

  • Anonymous usage statistics via Cloudflare Web Analytics (no cookies, no personal data, no tracking across sites)
  • Standard server logs (IP addresses, browser type, timestamps) retained by our hosting provider for security purposes

3. Cookies

ESRF.net does not use cookies or local storage to track visitors. Cloudflare Web Analytics is privacy-first and does not use cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare Pages — hosting and CDN
  • Cloudflare Web Analytics — anonymous, privacy-first analytics
  • OpenStreetMap / CARTO — map tiles (their privacy policies apply when loading map data)

5. Contact Information

When you contact us via office@esrf.net, we process your email address and message content solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not share this data with third parties.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of any personal data we may hold. Since we collect minimal data, this primarily applies to email correspondence. Contact us at office@esrf.net to exercise your rights.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
