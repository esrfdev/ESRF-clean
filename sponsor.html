    § 02 · Why sponsor

    Built on independence, sustained by patronage.

    ESRF.net is editorially independent. We accept no government mandates and no pay-to-rank listings. The directory is curated by a small team of practitioners who know this space — and who intend to keep it that way. While we display minimal, non-intrusive advertising to sustain operations, the true engine behind this network is transparent patronage from organisations who believe in the mission.

    ESRF.net reaches security and resilience professionals across 29 European nations — procurement managers, policy advisers, crisis coordinators, and the business leaders who staff the organisations in our directory. A sponsorship aligns your brand with Europe's most purposeful network in this field.

    We publish transparently: every sponsor is named, every tier disclosed. ESRF.net's editorial positions are never influenced by commercial relationships. If that distinction matters to your organisation, we are the right partner.

    Three ways to be part of it.

    Highest tier
    Founding Patron

    "Aligned with a vital sector of your choice."

    • Linked sponsor profile page
    Entry tier
    Community Supporter

    "Supporting the mission, acknowledged for it."
