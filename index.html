ABOUT THE FOUNDATION

European Security &
Resilience Framework

“Leadership by Example — for Local Impact and Resilience.”

The European Security & Resilience Framework is the foundation of ESRF.net — a decentralised network built for and by entrepreneurs with local impact.

The foundation channels donations, contributions and aligned capital into initiatives that strengthen the network and expand its reach across Europe.

We believe security is not just a government mandate — it is an entrepreneurial responsibility. By building resilience from the ground up, we connect organisations across 15 countries into self-sustaining ecosystems with regional impact.

Every member contributes to collective resilience through the five pillars: Vigilance, Stewardship, Empowerment, Solidarity, and Renewal.
