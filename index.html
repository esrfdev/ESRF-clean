    § 01 · The Foundation

    Leadership
    by example.

    For local impact. For shared resilience. For a Europe that holds.

    ESRF.net is a decentralised network built for, and by, the entrepreneurs with local impact. Funded by Stichting ESRF European Security and Resilience Fund, we believe that security is not just a mandate for governments — it is the shared work of practitioners, innovators and communities who strengthen Europe through cooperation every day.

    Security is not just a mandate. It is a responsibility we carry, together.

    By building resilience from the ground up, we connect organisations in thirty countries into self-sustaining ecosystems with regional impact. The foundation channels donations, contributions and aligned capital towards initiatives that strengthen the network and expand its reach. When many contribute a little, the result is greater than any single investment.

    Organisations are classified across ten vital sectors aligned with the EU NIS2 Directive and guided by five moral principles: vigilance, stewardship, empowerment, solidarity and renewal.
