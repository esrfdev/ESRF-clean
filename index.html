About the Foundation

European Security & Resilience Fund

The European Security & Resilience Fund is the financial backbone of ESRF.net — a decentralised network built for and by entrepreneurs with local impact. The foundation channels donations, contributions and aligned capital into initiatives that strengthen the network and expand its reach across Europe.

We believe in leadership by example: building resilience from the ground up, connecting organisations across 15 countries, and proving that security is not just a government mandate — it is an entrepreneurial responsibility.

Our mission is to create a self-sustaining ecosystem where every member contributes to collective resilience through the five pillars: Prevent, Protect, Prepare, Respond, and Recover.
