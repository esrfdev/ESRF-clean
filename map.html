    About these tags

    Tags describe the broad public function of an organisation — not its operational readiness, capacity, inventories, rosters, or deployment status. Use them to discover public-facing organisations and themes, not to infer emergency capability.

    For urgent help, always contact official emergency channels (112 in the EU, or your national service) — not this atlas.
